Battery Warranty Policy

Proration is calculated by total days from original battery purchase divided by 30 days.

Installation cost is fixed at $37.50.

Proration Tiers:

  • 0–24 months: Free Replacement (0%)
  • 25–32 months: 25% of Battery Cost (Customer Pay) + Battery Installation Labor
  • 33–50 months: 50% of Battery Cost (Customer Pay) + Battery Installation Labor
  • 51–84 months: 75% of Battery Cost (Customer Pay) + Battery Installation Labor
